\definesubsection{Newline}
\defineidentifier{newline}{-/-}
\definetextualcomponent{newline}{display: newline} \\

Newline is a \g{entity inline directive}. The following text in the \g{resulting textual component} should start on a new \g{line}. \\

\begin{examples}
  \example{This-/-and that}{This\\and that}
\end{examples}
