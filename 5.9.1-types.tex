\definesubsubsection{Embed Types}
An \g{implementation} must at least support the types specified in this section if permitted by the output \g{document}, but may add additional options the implications of which are completely \g{implementation dependant}. If the output \g{document} does not support a particular type, no \g{resulting textual component} is outputted in place of the \glink{match}{matched} embed. \\
