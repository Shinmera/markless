\definesubsection{Supertext}
\defineidentifier{supertext}{^<start [(]+><content ![(].*><start>}
\definetextualcomponent{supertext}{vertical-align: super} \\

Supertext is a \g{surrounding inline directive}. It marks the \g{text} to belong to a \g{textual component} that sets the style of the text to appear smaller and above the default text line. Only the \g{text} held by the \g{content binding} is outputted to the \g{resulting textual component}. \\
